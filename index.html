Our Juices (Cold‑Pressed)

Sorrel • Ginger

A citrus‑spice refresher with a warm, golden finish.

Highlights: Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants; turmeric (curcumin) and ginger (gingerols) are widely studied for anti‑inflammatory and digestive support. A clean, zesty pick‑me‑up for mid‑day slumps.

Orange • Apple • Turmeric • Ginger

A bright, fruit-forward blend with a warm, earthy lift.

Highlights: Naturally high in vitamin C and immune-supportive phytonutrients; apples provide gentle sweetness and polyphenols, while turmeric (curcumin) and ginger (gingerols) are known for anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits. A smooth, energizing boost for any time of day.

Beet • Carrot • Ginger

Earthy‑sweet and energizing with a gentle ginger kick.

Highlights: Naturally provides beta‑carotene, vitamin C, and folate. Popular for supporting stamina and everyday vitality; ginger adds soothing digestive support.

Pineapple • Cucumber • Ginger

Tropical and ultra‑refreshing.

Highlights: Pineapple brings vitamin C and bromelain (a digestive enzyme); cucumber adds hydrating crispness; ginger supports digestion and helps settle the stomach.
